Q & A

1. VXT’s Touch Panel is designed for what application?

Our Pcap Touch Panel can be applied on Panel PC, Medical Station, Industrial Control and other applications need durable and accurate input device.

2. What size of Touch Panel can VXT offer?

We can provide 3.5” to 32” Pcap Touch Panel, and we have a series of standard products.

3. Can VXT’s Touch Panel perform Multi-Touch and support Gesture Operation?

All our Touch Panel products are Multi-Touch device and, of course, they can support Gesture.
Customer can ask us to change the input detection from 10 points to 1 point if necessary.

4. Does VXT’s Touch Panel work well under thick Cover Glass?

VXT’s Touch Panel are designed to work in most circumstances, they can work under 1.1mm to 6mm thick Cover Glass

5. What OS can VXT’s Touch Panel work with? Has driver support?

Our VPH series Touch Panel can work in all OS in market, and we can provide driver to customer.
